How It Moves

From coast and farm to crate, same day.

01 — Sourcing

Landed and gathered fresh

Fish landed daily along Badagry's coastline; cassava and produce brought in from partner farms across the region.

02 — Processing

Cleaned, smoked, fried or baked

Each line is handled to its own standard — fish cleaned and smoked, cassava fermented into garri, dough baked into bread.

03 — Quality Check

Inspected before it leaves

Every batch is checked for freshness, hygiene and grade ahead of packaging.

04 — Dispatch

Sealed, packed and moved out

Orders are packaged and dispatched to markets and bulk buyers across Lagos and beyond.

Our Roots

Rooted in Badagry, built for Lagos.

Badagry's coastline has fed Lagos for generations — fishing crews at dawn, cassava fields inland, bread ovens running through the night. Bikkurim Foods Limited was built inside that rhythm, not apart from it.

We run fish, garri, bread and produce through one facility at 1-3 Isamole Road, off Oko Afo – Agunmo Road, so what's landed or harvested in the morning can be processed, checked and dispatched the same day.
